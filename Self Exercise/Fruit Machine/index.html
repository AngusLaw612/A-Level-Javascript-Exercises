Fruit Machine

Balance:

£1

The player starts with £1 credit, with each go costing 20p. If the Fruit Machine rolls two of the same symbol,
the player wins 50p. The player wins £1 for three of the same symbol and £5 for three Bells.
The player loses £1 if two Skulls are rolled and loses all their money if three Skulls are rolled.
After each roll, the player can choose to quit with their winnings or keep playing until there is no money left.
